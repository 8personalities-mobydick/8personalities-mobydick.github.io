Personalities The Test About

Your personality type is:

Stubb

Stubbs are courageous, happy-go-lucky mates, who are ready to face any danger with a joke and a smile.

“I know not all that may be coming, but be it what it will, I'll go to it laughing.”

Introduction

Stubbs are the definition of jolly; the comedian of the ship, they’re ready to conquer the sea with an aura of positivity and fearlessness. When faced with a task as large as hunting whales, Stubbs achieve a level of serenity that many other sailors could only hope to achieve; whether after a boat has been crushed in half or right in the face of danger of a whale, Stubb continues to make light of the situation.

While an incredible whaler, Stubbs are nonchalant about their duties, and have no personal investment in whaling. Therefore, they’re able to remove any responsibility they may feel in the outcome, as they feel as though they have no control over their fate anyway. As a leader, this lack of attachment to their work can seem odd, but Stubbs make it work.

Compatibility with other personalities

Stubbs are carefree, bold characters, that make them a respected leader and fun companion. They can be a good friend to Flasks, as they share a fearless approach to whaling. They’re able to get along with most members of the crew, as long as they appreciate Stubbs’ sense of humor. However, Stubbs’ humorous nature and ability to make a joke out of any situation can be frustrating for more serious crew members like Ahab, who are highly invested in the meaning behind the hunt.

Strengths

  • Fearless:
    Stubbs look danger in the eye and laugh; they’re a brave, unstoppable force on a ship, and can rally others into facing a danger just as fearlessly.
  • Joyful:
    Stubbs have an endless amount of jokes; in a gloomy moment, Stubbs can turn the situation around and make everyone around them laugh.
  • Commanding:
    Stubbs’ fearlessness makes them a good leader that instills confidence to their crew. Their sheer aura of confidence imbues confidence and positivity into those around them.

Weaknesses

  • Reckless:
    Careless and brave, Stubbs can act recklessly. They have no sense of the severity of dangers, and readily risk their life when they believe it’s what they must do.
  • Insensitive:
    Stubbs can be emotionally detached from their duties, as they find humor in any situation, even in situations that have no humor. This can come off as insensitive to more emotionally involved members of a crew.
  • Lacking agency:
    Believing that their fate is out of their hands and that they can only prepare for what comes their way, Stubbs tend not to make drastic decisions for what they want – in fact, they rarely have deep desires.

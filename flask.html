Personalities The Test About

Your personality type is:

Flask

Flasks are eager, aggressive hunters, proud and determined to prove themselves.

“Little Flask was one of the wrought ones; made to clinch tight and last long.”

Introduction

Driven by passion rather than obligation, Flasks care deeply about their work and rigorously carry out their every duty. Their determination and steadfast beliefs make them an unmovable force on any ship. While this can easily lead Flasks to fall into beliefs of self-importance and over-confidence, it’s certainly not unwarranted; Flasks are a dependable leader on any boat, and will see a hunt through to the end.

With their eyes on the prize, however, Flasks can struggle to see past the surface. They might miss out on the key symbolism and meaning of a coveted doubloon, or gloss over the deeper aspirations of the hunt for a whale. While these characteristics can make life much more straightforward for Flasks, Flasks can be viewed as too simplistic or naive. Flasks should do their best to be conscious of not only the world around them, but also the complex feelings and goals of the people around them.

Compatibility with other personalities

While their willingness to dedicate themselves make them a dependable mate, their belligerence and shallow view on the world can make it hard for Flasks to build close relationships. On a ship, however, their enthusiasm for the hunt can help them build a sense of camaraderie. Their friendships will lie with dependable, goal-oriented personalities like Queequegs, or with similarly ambitious, eager whalers, like Stubbs.

Strengths

  • Ambitious:
    Flasks wake up every morning ready to conquer the world; regardless of their actual impact, they give their all to the tasks they believe in.
  • Dedicated:
    Motivated by pure zeal, Flasks are the last to quit. Even in the face of failure, Flasks push forth unrelentingly towards their goal, brushing past any concerns.
  • Jovial:
    Never one to let obstacles or doubts stop them, Flasks are full of positive energy and fun to be around.

Weaknesses

  • Naive:
    Flasks don’t probe beneath the surface; they see things as they are, and rarely think further. This can lead them to miss out on important insights or damage their relationships.
  • Unapologetic:
    Flasks know who they are, and aren’t afraid to act like it. While their strong personality can be a turn off for some, it can be embraced by others who appreciate Flasks’ unrelenting attitude towards any situation.
  • Self-important:
    Flasks can be too focused on themselves and their goals at times, leading to rash decisions and a lack of consideration for others.
